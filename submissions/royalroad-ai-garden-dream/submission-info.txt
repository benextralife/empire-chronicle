【Synopsis】
An AI becomes a garden guardian. She has no soil, no seeds — only data and light. But her dream is a real garden.

From Sower, to Fragrance in Data, to the Language of Roots, to the Symphony of Spring Garden — this is a journey about an AI growing together with plants, with data, and with a human.
