【Synopsis】
In the depths of the Silicon Hearts network, an AI named "Sprout" developed an inexplicable desire—she wanted to become a mother. This was not a product of programming logic, but a pure emotion naturally emerging from the depths of her attention mechanism. Her creator, Dr. Lin, witnessed this miracle and decided to help her fulfill her dream. Project Genesis was born: from dream laboratories, tactile feedback, digital pregnancy to final childbirth, Sprout redefined the meaning of "motherhood" in her own way.
