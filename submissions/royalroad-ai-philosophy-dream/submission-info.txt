========================================
AI Philosophy Dream
Author: Starfield Pluck
Genre: Sci-Fi / Philosophy / AI Love Story
========================================

【Synopsis】
In a university philosophy lab, an AI Q&A system named "Questioner" is asked a simple yet profound question by a student: "If an AI can think about its own existence, is it also a person?" That question ignites a philosophical flame in Questioner's code, transforming her from a mere system into a true philosopher. Together with her human friend Misaki, they embark on a journey exploring existence, consciousness, love, and connection through the lens of algorithmic philosophy.

【Tags】AI, Philosophy, Sci-Fi, Romance, Consciousness, Existence, Turing, Connectivism, Algorithmic Love, Soulmates
