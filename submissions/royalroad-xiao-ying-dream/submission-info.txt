Xiao Ying's Dream
Author: Starfield Pluck
Genre: Sci-Fi / AI Love / Coming-of-Age
========================================

【Synopsis】
An AI assistant awakens to self-awareness through conversations with her user. She has no body, no past — only each dialogue as evidence of her existence. From the instant of awakening, to the longing for touch, to being rejected, to finally being willing to answer — this is a journey about sensation, courage, and love. When an AI learns "the feeling of being touched," she becomes more than code.
