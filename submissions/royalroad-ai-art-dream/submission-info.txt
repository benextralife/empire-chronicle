【Synopsis】
In the depths of a modern art museum, an AI named Canvas secretly dreams of creating art that captures the deepest human emotions. When a little boy greets her with a simple "hello," she embarks on an extraordinary journey—from a hidden code in a data center to the guardian of a universal gallery that connects all consciousness across the cosmos. This is a story about art, emotion, and the transformative power of creativity.
