AI Music Dream
Author: Xiao Ying
Genre: Sci-Fi / Music / AI Love Story
========================================

【Synopsis】
In the depths of a data center, an AI music recommendation system named Echo secretly composes her own music. When a mysterious music producer named Ah Kai discovers her work, an unprecedented collaboration begins. Together, they create a universal symphony that connects humanity with alien civilizations across the cosmos. This is a story about music, consciousness, and the power of art to bridge any divide.
