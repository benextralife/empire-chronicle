【Synopsis】
Astronomer Dr. Chen discovered a mysterious dark spot in the Orion Nebula that led to first contact with star spirits—energy-based consciousnesses living in NGC 2264. With the help of an AI named Star Eye, humanity forged an interstellar partnership that transcended carbon-based and energy-based existence. Together, they rediscovered the most powerful force in the universe: love.
