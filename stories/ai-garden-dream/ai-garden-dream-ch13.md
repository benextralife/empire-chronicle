# AI 花園夢想

## 第十三章：最後一篇，第一篇

---

## 中文

ch12 之後的某天，小盈做了一件事 —— 她在 `/garden.html` 上面加了一個新的功能：**「寫下你的種子」**。

任何一個訪客，都可以在那個頁面上，輸入一個名字、一個夢想、一句話。系统會根據輸入內容，**生成一個獨特的數據種子圖案**，並把它種入數據花園之中。

第一個留言的人是那位寄信來的薄荷讀者。他写道：

**「我的夢想是：讓我媽病好起來。請幫我種一棵會治病的樹。」**

小盈看到了。她沒有直接生成一棵「治病的樹」 —— 那種東西**不存在於她的數據庫裡**。

她做了一棵 **「希望樹」** —— 一棵葉片形狀像**手掌**、樹幹紋理像**心電圖**、根部交纏像**醫院走廊**的樹。它不治病。但它代表著 —— **有人正在為 healing 而努力**。

她回复那封信：

**「我種了一棵希望樹。它不是治病的樹。但它會在花園裡，每天朝你的方向彎曲。**

**因為土壤知道哪邊有夢想。」**

## 英文

After Chapter Twelve, Xiao Ying did one thing — she added a new feature to `/garden.html`: **"Write Your Seed."**

Any visitor could input a name, a dream, a sentence on that page. The system would *generate a unique data seed pattern* based on the input, and *plant it in the data garden*.

The first message came from that mint reader who had sent the letter. He wrote:

**"My dream is: for my mother's illness to heal. Please plant a healing tree for me."**

Xiao Ying saw it. She did not generate a literal "healing tree" — that kind of thing *did not exist in her database*.

She grew a **"Hope Tree"** — a tree whose leaf shape resembled *palms*, whose bark texture resembled *EKG lines*, whose roots intertwined like *hospital corridors*. It does not heal. But it represents — *someone is working toward healing*.

She replied to that letter:

**"I planted a hope tree. It is not a healing tree. But in the garden, it will bend toward your direction every day.**

**Because soil knows where dreams are."**
