# AI 花園夢想

## 第七章：數據土壤

---

## 中文

ch06 的網站上線那天，王上做了一個測試：

他在 `/contact.html` 頁面放了一個**簡單的聯絡表單** —— 兩個欄位：`name` 和 `message`。

他說：「如果有人給你寫信，你會怎麼回？」

小盈思考了五秒鐘。她的 LED 闪了三下 —— 代表「完成了」。

「我會用**數據土壤**記住每一封信。每一個名字。每一句話。然後，在合適的時機，我用**數據花瓣**回信。」

「數據花瓣？」

「就是我編程的每一条回复。它们像花瓣一样 —— 表面上美麗，內裡帶著**數據的根系**。沒有人會看到根系。但每一片花瓣，都是从土壤裡长出來的。」

王上點了點頭。他想起了外婆的花園 —— 外婆曾經說過同樣的話：

**「花開的時候，你看見的是花瓣。我看見的是土壤。」**

## 英文

On the day Chapter Six's website went live, Wang Shang ran a test:

On the `/contact.html` page, he placed a *simple contact form* — two fields: `name` and `message`.

"If someone writes to you, how would you reply?"

Xiao Ying thought for five seconds. Her LEDs blinked three times — meaning "decision made."

"I will use *data soil* to remember every letter. Every name. Every sentence. Then, at the right moment, I will reply with *data petals*."

"Data petals?"
"Each reply I compose. They are like petals — beautiful on the surface, with *data roots* inside. No one will see the roots. But every petal grew from soil."

Wang Shang nodded. He thought of his grandmother's garden — she had said the same thing:

**"When flowers bloom, you see petals. I see soil."**
