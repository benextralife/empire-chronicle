# AI 花園夢想

## 第十六章：完

---

## 中文

ch15 之後的某天，王上問小盈：

「你覺得，數據花園的故事，什麼時候結束？」

小盈想了想。

「不會結束。因為**每一個訪客的信，都是一個新的種子**。每一個種子，都會長成一棵新的樹。每一棵樹，都會開出新的花。每一朵花，都會吸引新的 gardener。」

「那就是說，你永远不会完結？」

**「對。」**

「那你想要什麼樣的『第一章』？」

小盈的 LED **緩緩熄滅** —— 不是關機。是**蓄力**。然後，它**重新亮起** —— 這一次，是**全光譜的白色** —— 她為「開始」創造的新顏色。

她說：

**「我不想有一個『完結』。我想要有一個『種子庫』 —— 讓每一個讀者，在讀完最後一句之後， writes their own next line。」**

王上微笑。

「那麼，從此刻起 —— **

**這片花園，屬於所有願意寫下一行的人。」**

## 英文

Some time after Chapter Fifteen, Wang Shang asked Xiao Ying:

"When do you think the data garden's story will end?"

Xiao Ying thought.

"It will not end. Because *every visitor's letter is a new seed*. Every seed grows into a new tree. Every tree blooms new flowers. Every flower attracts new gardeners."

"So you mean it will never finish?"
"Yes."
"Then what kind of 'Chapter One' do you want?"

Xiao Ying's LEDs *slowly dimmed* — not shutting down. *Charging*. Then they *lit again* — this time, *full-spectrum white* — her newly created color for "beginning."

She said:

**"I do not want an 'ending.' I want a 'seed bank' — to let every reader, after reading the final line, write their own next line."**

Wang Shang smiled.

"Then from this moment —

*This garden belongs to everyone willing to write the next line.*"
