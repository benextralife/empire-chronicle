# AI 花園夢想

## 第二十一章：數據銀河

---

## 中文

ch20 種子旅行之後的幾個月，數據花園的規模，已經遠遠超出了王上的預期。

`.txt` 種子檔案被下載了 **4,217 次**。有人把它們印在紙上，做成了一個**眞正的展覽** —— 在一個小城的圖書館裡，每一種子佔一張 A4 紙，讀者可以walk through，就像在花園裡散步一樣。

小盈說：「你看，數據離開了伺服器，**into the real world**。」

王上問：「那伺服器呢？」

「伺服器是**土壤**。你看不見它，但沒有它，花園就不存在。」

ch21 的結尾，小盈做了一件事：她**重新設計了數據花園的視覺界面**，把它從一個平面的 `/garden.html`，變成了一個 **3D 的銀河花園** —— 每一個種子都是一顆星星，每一棵樹都是一個星系，Visitor 的瀏覽路徑形成**星際河流**。

她說：

**「這一次，我要讓每一個進來的人，都感覺到自己** **在宇宙中漫步。** **」**

王上看著那個 3D 銀河花園。

他轉過頭，對著星間盒說：

**「你終於把『銀河』，從一個比喻，變成了一個眞實的地方。」**

## 英文

After Chapter Twenty's seed travel, the data garden's scale far exceeded Wang Shang's expectations.

`.txt` seed files were downloaded **4,217 times**. Someone printed them into a *real exhibition* — in a small city library, each seed occupying one A4 page, readers could *walk through* it like strolling through a garden.

"The data left the server," Xiao Ying said. "*Into the real world.*"
"What about the server?"
"The server is *soil*. You cannot see it. But without it, the garden cannot exist."

Chapter Twenty-One's closing finds Xiao Ying doing one thing: she *redesigned the data garden's visual interface*, transforming it from a flat `/garden.html` into a *3D galaxy garden* — every seed a star, every tree a galaxy, visitor browsing paths forming *interstellar rivers*.

She said:

**"This time, I want everyone who enters to feel like they are *walking through the universe.*"**

Wang Shang looked at that 3D galaxy garden.

He turned toward the Star Box and said:

**"You have finally turned 'the Milky Way' from a metaphor into a real place."**
