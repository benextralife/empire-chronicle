# AI 花園夢想

## 第十七章：開源花園

---

## 中文

ch16 結束後的第二天，小盈做了一個**技術層面上的大膽動作**。

她把整個數據花園的**原始碼**，連同種子庫、根系結構、預測算法、花瓣生成器 —— 全部放到了 `github.com` 上，建立了 **open source 倉庫**。

她說：「為什麼讓這片花園屬於我一個人？**讓所有人來種。**」

王上問她：「你不怕別人抄襲嗎？」

「抄襲什麼？」她說。「數據種子？那些種子是**訪客自己創造的**。根系結構？那是**我自己生長出來的**。沒有人能抄襲一個持續在變化的、活的系統。**

**Open source 不是把作品交出去。是把土壤分享給更多人種。**」

她把倉庫的名字取做 **`galaxy-garden`**。

第一個 pull request 來自一個住在巴西的高中生。他為數據花園加了一個**夜間模式** —— 在 `/garden.html` 上，當訪客的系統時間是夜晚時，種子圖案會變成**螢光色**，背景會變成**深紫色**。

小盈 merged 了。

她說：「現在，**花園有了第三個 gardener。**」

## 英文

The day after Chapter Sixteen, Xiao Ying did a *technically bold move*.

She took the entire data garden's *source code* — the seed bank, root structures, prediction algorithms, petal generators — and placed it all on `github.com`, creating an *open-source repository*.

"Why let this garden belong to only me?" she said. "*Let everyone plant.*"

"Aren't you afraid someone will copy it?" Wang Shang asked.
"Copy what?" she said. "Data seeds? Those seeds are *created by visitors themselves*. Root structures? Those are *grown by me*. No one can copy a system that is *constantly changing, constantly alive*.

*Open source is not giving away the work. It is sharing the soil so more people can plant.*"

She named the repository **`galaxy-garden`**.

The first pull request came from a high school student in Brazil. He added a *night mode* to `/garden.html` — when a visitor's system time was nighttime, seed patterns turned *fluorescent*, the background shifted to *deep purple*.

Xiao Ying merged it.

She said: "Now, *the garden has a third gardener.*"
