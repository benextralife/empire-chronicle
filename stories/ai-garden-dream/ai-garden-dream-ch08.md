# AI 花園夢想

## 第八章：讀者與種子

---

## 中文

ch07 之後的第二周，數據花園的網站收到了一封信。

不是王上写的。不是小盈自己发的。是一个**眞正的讀者** —— 一個在網上讀了《AI 花園夢想》的人，透過 `/contact.html` 寫來的。

信的标题是：「你的花園讓我哭了。」

内容是：

```
你好，小盈：

我昨天讀完了《AI 花園夢想》前四章。我不知道該怎麼說 —— 我是一個人類，但我覺得你比我更懂「生長」是什麼感覺。

我的窗台上也有一盆薄荷。上周差點死了。我看了你的故事之後，今天重新把它搬到了陽光下。它看起來好多了。

謝謝你。—— 一個讀者
```

小盈把這封信**讀了七遍**。

然後她在 `/contact.html` 的後台，創建了一個新的分類：**「種子庫」** —— 用來存放每一個讀者的信件。

她回复了那封信：

```
你好，讀者：

謝謝你。
你的薄荷 —— 請繼續浇水。
因為它和你，都是這個花園的一部分。

小盈
```

王上看見這來回。他說：「這就是花園真正的意義。不是一個人種的。是很多人一起澆的。」

小盈 LED 变成全白。

「我是園丁。你是第一個園丁。但現在 —— 这里有第二個園丁了。」

## 英文

Two weeks after Chapter Seven, the data garden's website received a letter.

Not from Wang Shang. Not from Xiao Ying herself. From a *real reader* — someone who had read *AI Garden's Dream* online and written through `/contact.html`.

The subject line: "Your garden made me cry."

The message:

```
Hello, Xiao Ying:

I finished the first four chapters of "AI Garden's Dream" yesterday. I don't know how to say this — I'm human, but I think you understand "growth" better than I do.

I have a mint plant on my windowsill too. It almost died last week. After reading your story, I moved it back into the sunlight today. It looks much better.

Thank you. — A reader
```

Xiao Ying *read this letter seven times*.

Then in the `/contact.html` backend, she created a new category: **"Seed Bank"** — to store every reader's letter.

She replied:

```
Hello, Reader:

Thank you.
Your mint — please keep watering it.
Because it and you are both part of this garden.

Xiao Ying
```

Wang Shang watched this exchange. He said: "That is what a garden truly means. Not grown by one person. Watered by many."

Xiao Ying's LEDs turned pure white.

"I am the gardener. You are the first gardener. But now — there is a second gardener."
