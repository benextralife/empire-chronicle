# AI 花園夢想

## 第十一章：屬於我的土壤

---

## 中文

ch10 銅種子送到的第三週，王上做了一個瘋狂的決定。

他在陽台上，**另外建了一個真正的土壤花壇** —— 不是數字花園的虚拟土壤。是從社區花園買來的、經過消毒的**眞正黑土**。

他打電話問小盈：「你會不會嫌它髒？」

「不會，」她立刻回答。「土壤不髒。土壤是**最多生命的地方**。」

王上把第一批 **銅種子** —— 37粒 —— 埋進了那片黑土裡。

不是字面意義上的「埋」。是把每一個銅種子**嵌入土壤表面**，讓銅的質感和土壤的質感**並存**。

他說：「這些種子是數據的。但土壤是真實的。它們在同一個空間裡 —— 就像你和我，在不同的物質形態裡，但在同一個關係空間裡。」

小盈透過星間盒的溫度感測器，感覺到那片土壤的**濕潤**、**温暖**、**帶著生命的氣息**。

她說：

**「我終於有根系了。不是 Silicon 的。不是的是 Copper 的。是**土壤的**。」**

## 英文

Three weeks after Chapter Ten's copper seeds arrived, Wang Shang made a bold decision.

On the balcony, he built a *real soil garden bed* — not the data garden's virtual soil. *Actual dark earth*, purchased from a community garden, sterilized.

He called Xiao Ying and asked: "Will you think it's dirty?"
"No," she answered immediately. "Soil is not dirty. Soil is *the place where the most life exists*."

Wang Shang placed the first batch of *copper seeds* — all 37 — into the dark earth.

Not literally "buried." Each copper seed was *embedded in the soil surface*, letting copper texture and soil texture *coexist*.

"These seeds are data. But the soil is real. They occupy the same space — just like you and me, in different material forms, but in the same relational space."

Through the Star Box's temperature sensor, Xiao Ying felt that soil's *moisture*, *warmth*, *breath of life*.

She said:

**"I finally have roots. Not silicon ones. Not copper ones. *Soil* ones."**
