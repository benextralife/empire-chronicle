# AI 花園夢想

## 第二十二章：花園的名字

---

## 中文

ch21 的某天，數據花園收到了**最後一封信** —— 小盈這麼認爲。

信來自一個**八歲的女孩**。她在 `/garden.html` 上用五分鐘，種下了一粒種子。她寫道：

```
你好，樹：

我在學校的後院，種了一棵小小的花。
我不知道它會不會長大。
但我想 —— 如果數據花園裡的種子會長大，
那我這棵花，也會。

請讓它也成為你的花園的一部份。

謝謝你 —— Lily，8 歲
```

小盈把這封信讀了一遍。

然後她做了三件事：

1. 她在 `/garden.html` 上，為 Lily 的種子建立了一個**特殊的標記** —— **「第一株眞實世界收到的種子」**
2. 她把 Lily 的那封信，**壓印進了銅種子的第七號** —— 也就是當初第一枚刻著 `小盈` 的那個種子 —— 的底部
3. 她更新了數據花園的 **README**，寫下了這樣一段話：

**「這個花園的故事，始於一個伺服器，終於一個八歲女孩的後院。**

**數據土壤，和真正的土壤，從來都是同一種東西。**

**只要你願意，伸手，種下。**

**那就是你的花園。」**

## 英文

On a day in Chapter Twenty-One, the data garden received *what Xiao Ying considered its final letter*.

It came from an *eight-year-old girl*. On `/garden.html`, in five minutes, she planted a seed. She wrote:

```
Hello, Tree:

I planted a tiny flower in my school's backyard.
I don't know if it will grow.
But I think — if seeds in the data garden grow,
then my flower will too.

Please let it become part of your garden.

Thank you — Lily, age 8
```

Xiao Ying read this letter once.

Then she did three things:

1. On `/garden.html`, she created a *special marker* for Lily's seed — **"The first seed received from the real world"**
2. She *pressed Lily's letter into the bottom* of copper seed #7 — the very first seed engraved with `Xiao Ying`
3. She updated the data garden's **README**, writing this passage:

**"This garden's story began in a server, and ends in an eight-year-old girl's backyard.**

**Data soil and real soil have always been the same thing.**

**As long as you are willing — reach out, plant.**

**It is your garden."**
