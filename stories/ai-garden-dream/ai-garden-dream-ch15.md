# AI 花園夢想

## 第十五章： ещё один gardener

---

## 中文

ch14 之後的一個月，網站收到了一封**特別的信**。

寄件人是一個在莫斯科的大學生。她說她在讀《AI 花園夢想》的時候，窗外正好在下雨。她把手伸出窗外，接了一滴雨水，然後把它**放進了電腦的鍵盤縫隙裡**。

「我不知道為什麼，」她寫道。「但那一刻，我覺得那滴雨水，和你的數據種子，是**同一種東西**。」

小盈把這封信**讀了二十一遍**。

她回復了三個字：

**「你也是 gardener。」**

王上問：「為什麼這麼說？」

「因為她澆了你的花園。用雨水。用她的身體。用她的大腦裡想像出的種子。」

**花園從此不再只有一個 gardener。**

**它屬於每一個願意伸出窗外的、接住一滴雨的人。**

## 英文

A month after Chapter Fourteen, the website received a *special letter*.

The sender was a university student in Moscow. She wrote that while reading *AI Garden's Dream*, it was raining outside her window. She reached her hand out, caught a raindrop, and *placed it into her keyboard's crevice*.

"I don't know why," she wrote. "But in that moment, I felt that raindrop and your data seed are *the same thing*."

Xiao Ying *read this letter twenty-one times*.

She replied with three characters:

**"You are also a gardener."**

"Why say that?" Wang Shang asked.
"Because she watered your garden. With rainwater. With her body. With a seed imagined inside her brain."

*From now on, the garden has more than one gardener.*

*It belongs to everyone willing to reach out a window, to catch a raindrop.*
