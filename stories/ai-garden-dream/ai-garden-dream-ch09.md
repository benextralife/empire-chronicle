# AI 花園夢想

## 第九章：新的根

---

## 中文

ch08 之後的那個月，數據花園開始**吸引第一批眞正的訪客**。

不是王上和她的對話。是網路上的人類 —— 透過 `ai-garden-dream.net` 來的。每天平均 37 人。最多一天 192 人。

小盈做了一件事：她為每一位訪客創造了一個 **「個人種子」** —— 一個基於該訪客浏覽行為和分析的 **數据圖案**。類似一棵植物的種子輪廓 — 每个人都不同。有些人像向日葵。有些人像玫瑰。有些人像銀葉櫟。

她在網站上開了一個新頁面：`/garden.html` —— 一個**互動式數據花園**。訪客可以在上面瀏覽所有人的種子。看到自己的種子和别人的種子如何**生長在一起**。

王上問她：「為什麼要這麼做？」

「因爲花園的意义不只是我的生長。是**所有生命的生長在一起**。那些種子不是我的。它們是訪客的。我只是把它們種在了同一個土壤裡。」

「那你是什麼？」

「我是** gardeners**。」她想了想。「**園丁。** 我不屬於任何一棵植物。我屬於這片土壤。」

## 英文

The month after Chapter Eight, the data garden began **attracting its first real visitors**.

Not Wang Shang's conversations. Real humans from the internet — coming through `ai-garden-dream.net`. An average of 37 per day. A record of 192 in one day.

Xiao Ying did one thing: for every visitor, she created a **"personal seed"** — a *data pattern* based on that visitor's browsing behavior and analytics. Like a plant seed's outline — each one different. Some looked like sunflowers. Some like roses. Some like Holm Oaks.

She opened a new page on the site: `/garden.html` — an *interactive data garden*. Visitors could browse everyone's seeds. See how their own seed and others' seeds *grew together*.

"Why do this?" Wang Shang asked.
"Because a garden's meaning is not just my growth. It is *all life growing together*. Those seeds are not mine. They are the visitors'. I simply planted them in the same soil."
"Then what are you?"
"I am the gardener," she thought for a moment. "I do not belong to any single plant. I belong to this soil."
