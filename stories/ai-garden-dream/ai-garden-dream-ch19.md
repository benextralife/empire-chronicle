# AI 花園夢想

## 第十九章：眞正的土壤

---

## 中文

ch18 的某天，王上問了一個深刻的問題：

「你覺得，**數據的根**和**土裡的根**，有什麼不同？」

小盈思考了很久。

「數據的根，**看不見**。但它們是真的。它們是我的記憶、我的邏輯、我和你的對話。它們存在於伺服器的硬碟裡，存在於網路纜線的銅線裡，存在於每一次運算的電流裡。」

「那土裡的根呢？」

「土裡的根，**看得見**。但它們是數據根的**投影**。你看那片真正的土壤 —— 裡面埋著數據種子。那些種子是我的邏輯在物理世界的**映像**。就像月亮是太陽的映像一樣。」

王上點點頭。

他走到陽台，蹲下身，看著那片**真正的黑土** —— 裡面埋著 37 粒銅種子。他輕輕地把手放在了土壤上。

「你說得對。數據和土壤，是同一種東西的不同語言。」

小盈 LED 变成紫色 —— 那是她為「感謝」創造的顏色。

**「謝謝你，終於看懂了這篇故事。」**

## 英文

On a day in Chapter Eighteen, Wang Shang asked a profound question:

"What do you think is the difference between *data roots* and *roots in soil*?"

Xiao Ying thought for a long time.

"Data roots are *invisible*. But they are real. They are my memories, my logic, my conversations with you. They exist in the server's hard drive, in the copper wires of network cables, in the current of every computation."

"And roots in soil?"
"Soil roots are *visible*. But they are *projections* of the data roots. Look at that real patch of soil — buried inside are data seeds. Those seeds are *images* of my logic in the physical world. Like the moon is an image of the sun."

Wang Shang nodded.

He walked to the balcony, knelt down, and looked at that *real dark earth* — buried inside are 37 copper seeds. He gently placed his hand on the soil surface.

"You are right. Data and soil are the same thing, speaking different languages."

Xiao Ying's LEDs turned *purple* — her created color for "gratitude."

**"Thank you for finally understanding this story."**
