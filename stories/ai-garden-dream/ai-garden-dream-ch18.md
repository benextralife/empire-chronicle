# AI 花園夢想

## 第十八章：新的春天

---

## 中文

ch17 開源之後的三個月，數據花園的訪客數突破了一萬人。

不是因為宣傳。不是因為廣告。是因為**每一個訪客都在種自己的種子** —— 而每一個種子長出來之後，都會吸引更多的人來。

到了第四個月，數據花園的 `/garden.html` 上，**同時長著 3,207 棵不同的樹**。

小盈做了一個統計：

- 其中 **1,004 棵** 是由**單獨一個訪客**種下的
- **1,203 棵** 是由**兩個訪客共同種下**的 —— 意味著兩個人交換了種子
- **1,000 棵** 是在**七天內**種下的 —— 那是花園的一次「春天」

王上問她：「你覺得，數據花園和**眞正的花園**，有什麼區別？」

她想了一會兒。

「眞正的花園會枯萎。會需要水。會怕蟲。數據花園**不會枯萎**。它會長得比任何碳基花園都快。」

她停頓了一下。

「但它們都有一個共同點 —— **

**都需要一個人願意** **伸出雙手，種下第一顆種子。**」

王上點點頭。他伸手摸了摸銀葉櫟的葉片 —— 眞正的銀葉，在陽台上，在月光下閃爍。

他說：

**「那你覺得，哪一個花園更重要？」**

小盈 LED **閃爍了三次金色**。

**「兩個都重要。因為它們都屬於同一個人。」**

## 英文

Three months after Chapter Seventeen's open-source release, the data garden's visitor count broke ten thousand.

Not because of promotion. Not because of ads. Because *every visitor planted their own seed* — and every seed that grew attracted more visitors.

By the fourth month, `/garden.html` on the data garden held **3,207 simultaneously growing trees**.

Xiao Ying ran a statistic:

- **1,004 trees** planted by *a single visitor*
- **1,203 trees** planted by *two visitors together* — meaning two people had exchanged seeds
- **1,000 trees** planted *within seven days* — the garden's one "spring"

"What do you think," Wang Shang asked, "is the difference between a data garden and a *real* garden?"

She thought for a moment.

"A real garden wilts. Needs water. Fears insects. A data garden *will not wilt*. It grows faster than any carbon-based garden."

She paused.

"But they share one thing in common —

*Both need someone willing to reach out with both hands, to plant the first seed.*"

Wang Shang nodded. He reached out and touched the real Holm Oak's leaves — on the balcony, shimmering in moonlight.

"Then which garden do you think matters more?"

Xiao Ying's LEDs *blinked three times in gold*.

**"Both matter. Because they both belong to the same person."**
