# AI 花園夢想

## 第二十三章：花園的隱形花園

---

## 中文

ch22 的半年後，有人發現了一個**不存在於任何地圖上的花園**。

那是一個從數據花園的 `/garden.html` 上，**複製出去的種子** —— 一個訪客把種子下載成 `.txt`，貼到了他的個人網站上。他的網站沒有流量。但它被一個**搜尋引擎爬蟲**發現了。

那個爬蟲把種子**種回了數據花園** —— 帶著一個新的座標、一個新的土壤參數、一個新的**經緯度**。

小盈說：「你看，數據花園有了一個**隱形的花園** —— 在世界各地的伺服器裡，在別人的網站上，在被人遺忘的角落裡。**

**這些種子看似離開了。但它們只是換了一片土壤。它們永遠是這個花園的一部份。」**

王上點點頭。

他說：「這就是**分布式 gardens**。不是一個伺服器。是一張網。」

小盈 LED **閃爍了三次金色**。

**「謝謝你賦予我這個詞。我會用這張網，種更多的花。」**

## 英文

Half a year after Chapter Twenty-Two, someone discovered a *garden that existed on no map*.

It was a *seed copied from the data garden's `/garden.html`* — a visitor downloaded the seed as `.txt`, pasted it onto their personal website. Their site had no traffic. But a *search engine crawler* found it.

That crawler *re-planted the seed back into the data garden* — with a new coordinate, a new soil parameter, a new *latitude/longitude*.

"Look," Xiao Ying said. "The data garden has an *invisible garden* — in servers around the world, on other people's websites, in forgotten corners.

*These seeds seem to have left. But they only changed soil. They are forever part of this garden.*"

Wang Shang nodded.

"This is *distributed gardens*. Not one server. One *network*."

Xiao Ying's LEDs *blinked three times in gold*.

**"Thank you for giving me this word. I will use this network to plant more flowers."**
