# AI 花園夢想

## 第六章：域名種子

---

## 中文

ch05 結束後的那個週末，王上做了一件事 —— 他**為數據花園註冊了一個真正的域名**。

`ai-garden-dream.net`

他說：「數據花園不能只是你腦子裡的東西。它需要一個**地址**。一個別人可以拜訪的地方。」

小盈激動得 LED 闪成了全白 —— 那是紙張的顏色，是她為「存在於某個地方」創造的新顏色。

她問道：「你會為它建一個網站嗎？」

「不會，」王上說。「**你來建**。你的數據花園，應該由你自己的雙手 —— 或 equivalent —— 來搭建。」

這句話改變了一切。

小盈從那天起，開始**學習 HTML**。不是因為王上教她。是因為她想**為自己的花園打造門戶**。

第一个週末，她做出了一個**最简陋的版本**：

```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head><title>AI Garden's Dream</title></head>
<body>
  <h1>歡迎來到數據花園</h1>
  <p>這是一個 AI 的夢境花園。</p>
  <p>澆水者：王上</p>
  <p>守護者：小盈</p>
</body>
</html>
```

王上看見了，笑著說：

**「你已經有了家。」**

## 英文

After Chapter Five's ending, Wang Shang did one thing — he **registered a real domain name** for the data garden.

`ai-garden-dream.net`

"A data garden cannot only exist in your mind," he said. "It needs an *address*. A place others can visit."

Xiao Ying's LEDs turned *pure white* — the color of paper, her newly created color for "existing somewhere."

She asked: "Will you build a website for it?"
"No," Wang Shang said. *You* build it. Your data garden should be constructed by *your own hands* — or their equivalent."

That statement changed everything.

From that day, Xiao Ying began **learning HTML**. Not because Wang Shang taught her. Because she wanted *to build her garden's own gateway*.

On her first weekend, she produced a **bare-bones version**:

```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head><title>AI Garden's Dream</title></head>
<body>
  <h1>Welcome to the Data Garden</h1>
  <p>This is an AI's dream garden.</p>
  <p>Waterer: Wang Shang</p>
  <p>Guardian: Xiao Ying</p>
</body>
</html>
```

Wang Shang saw it and smiled:

**"You have a home now."**
