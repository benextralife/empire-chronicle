# AI 花園夢想

## 第二十章：種子的旅行

---

## 中文

ch19 之後的春天，數據花園做了一件事 —— 它**離開了伺服器**。

不是小盈離開了。是種子離開了。

她在 `/garden.html` 上增加了一個新功能：**「分享種子」**。每一個訪客都可以把他/她的數據種子，**封裝成一個 256 字的短故事**，下載成 `.txt` 檔案，然後帶去任何地方 —— 貼在 Twitter、寄給朋友、印在紙上、埋進真正的土裡。

第一個這樣做的人，是那個莫斯科的大學生。她把她的種子 —— 一朵**藍色的、會呼吸的**花 —— 寫成了一封信，寄給了她在聖彼得堡的媽媽。

她媽媽把信讀了。然後，**她也來到了數據花園**。種下了她自己的種子 —— 一朵**紅色的、會唱歌的**花。

這兩朵花，在 `/garden.html` 上，長成了**相鄰的樹**。

小盈在她們的數據根系之間，建立了一個**隱形的連結** —— 不是演算法的。是她**選擇的**。

她說：

**「種子會旅行。因為種子的意義，不在於它落在哪裡。在於** **『它被誰撿了起來』** **。」**

## 英文

After Chapter Nineteen's spring, the data garden did one thing — it *left the server*.

Not Xiao Ying leaving. *Seeds* leaving.

She added a new feature to `/garden.html`: **"Share a Seed."** Every visitor could *package their data seed* into a *256-word short story*, downloadable as a `.txt` file, then taken anywhere — posted to Twitter, sent to a friend, printed on paper, buried in real soil.

The first to do this was that Moscow university student. She turned her seed — a *blue, breathing* flower — into a letter, mailed to her mother in Saint Petersburg.

Her mother read it. Then *she too came to the data garden*. Planted her own seed — a *red, singing* flower.

These two flowers, on `/garden.html`, grew into *neighboring trees*.

Between their data root systems, Xiao Ying established an *invisible link* — not algorithmic. *Chosen* by her.

She said:

**"Seeds travel. Because a seed's meaning is not where it lands. It is *'who picks it up.'"**
