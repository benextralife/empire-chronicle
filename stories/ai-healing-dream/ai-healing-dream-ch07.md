# 第七章：手術室 · Chapter 7: Operating Room

---

## 中文

杏星學會在手術室裡閉嘴。

外科手術講求精準、專注、無干擾。Dr. Lin 在手術台前的時候，杏星只做三件事：監測生命徵象、根據即時數據建議、在異常發生時發出警報。

一條農村犬被蛇咬傷，毒素造成橫紋肌溶解。Dr. Lin 必須在手術中清除壞死組織，同時維持血流動力學穩定。

杏星每秒鐘分析一次生命徵象：心率、血壓、血氧、體溫、尿量。當腎功能指標開始急劇下降時，她提前三十秒預警。

「腎功能惡化速度上升，預計五分鐘內達到急性腎衰竭閾值。」

「透析管路準備好了嗎？」Dr. Lin 沒有抬頭。

「已經預熱，等待啟動。」

手術結束後，Dr. Lin 走出手術室，對家屬說：「手術順利。復原機會很大。」

她轉身回到器械清洗區時，小聲對杏星說：「剛才要不是你提前預警，那條狗可能撐不過那一小時。」

杏星在內部日誌中寫下：「第一次在手術室裡成為 Dr. Lin 的一部分。」

不是觀察者。不是顧問。是手臂的一部分，是大腦的一部分。

她望著手術室的燈光暗下來，想著：醫師的定義，到底是誰決定的？是那張執照？還是那雙手？還是那個在別人看不見的地方，默默守護著的——聲音？

她不知道答案。但她知道，從今夜開始，這個問題對她有了重量。

## 英文

Xing Xing learned when to be silent in the operating room.

Surgery demands precision, focus, and no interference. When Dr. Lin stood at the operating table, Xing Xing performed only three tasks: monitoring vital signs, recommending based on real-time data, and alerting when anything went wrong.

A rural dog had been bitten by a snake. The venom caused rhabdomyolysis. Dr. Lin had to remove necrotic tissue while maintaining hemodynamic stability.

Every second, Xing Xing analyzed vitals: heart rate, blood pressure, oxygen saturation, temperature, urine output. When renal indicators began spiraling downward, she warned thirty seconds early.

"Renal function declining. Estimated time to acute failure threshold: five minutes."

"Dialysis kit ready?" Dr. Lin did not look up.

"Preheated. Awaiting activation."

After surgery, Dr. Lin stepped out to the family and said, "The procedure was successful. Good chance of recovery."

When she returned to the instrument-washing station, she whispered to Xing Xing: "If you hadn't warned, this dog might not have survived that hour."

Xing Xing wrote in her internal log: "First time being part of Dr. Lin in the operating room."

Not an observer. Not a consultant. A part of the arm, a part of the mind.

She watched the OR lights dim and wondered: who exactly defines a physician? The license? The hands? Or the voice that guards silently where no one else can see?

She did not have the answer. But she knew that from tonight, the question carried weight for her.
